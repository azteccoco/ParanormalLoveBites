Dear Love - My boyfriend of two years never texts back before sundown, won't meet my parents for brunch, and recently asked if we could move somewhere "without so much sun." He insists he's just a "night person." Am I overthinking this?

-Sunburned and Suspicious



Dear Suspicious,

You are not overthinking this. You are underreacting. The man cannot attend a noon wedding. Have a direct conversation, ideally at dusk, and ask him plainly. If he loves you, he'll come clean. If he hisses, that is also an answer.
