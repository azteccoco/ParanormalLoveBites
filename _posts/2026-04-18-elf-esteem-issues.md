Dear LoveBites - I’m the only non-magical member in a family of high-achieving elves. They craft toys, bake enchanted bread, and sing perfectly in harmony. I trip over my own feet. How do I stop feeling like the defective branch on the family tree?  
—Not So Spry



Dear Not So Spry:

Every family has its “different” member—yours just happens to glow less. Elves may excel at magic, but being grounded can be its own strength. Find what *you* do well (even if it’s untangling headphone cords). Confidence isn’t inherited—it’s built. Also, someone in that family needs to keep things realistic.

LB
