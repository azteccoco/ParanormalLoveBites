Dear Love,

This vampire I like keeps texting me at 2am asking if I’m ‘up’… is this a situation-ship or could this be a hint for something more? 

-Casually Dating

&nbsp;

Dear Casually,

It’s giving *“you up?”* energy*,* not *“I see a future with you”* energy*.*  If all their effort lives between midnight and sunrise, that’s not mystery, that’s a pattern. Yes, they’re a vampire, but even vampires can plan ahead, hold conversations that aren’t convenience-based, and show interest outside of “are you available right now.”

Right now, you’re being treated like an option, not a priority.

LB
